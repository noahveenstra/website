Knitwrth
Case Studies / Websites
Knitwrth is an apparel startup that has amassed a growing audience of 30k+ followers along with appearances in Vouge, Bazaar and Chicago Mag. They approached me in order to get their website off of a third party hosting site. I document my process in figuring out and learning on the way of making an e-commerce site from scratch.
Apple Muisc
Case Studies / Mobile Apps
Apple Music has so much potential to be the best on demand music stream service out there. Although, there are a few key features that are just missing for this premier service. I take a look into how some changes will affect its success and popularity using peer reviewed research papers and present my findings in high fidelity mock-ups.
