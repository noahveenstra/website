Who am I?
UX Architect / UI Designer / Web Developer / Techie / Sports Enthusiast

Growing up, I kind of knew I would end up somewhere around technology for the rest of my life. My dad worked as a software developer therefore I was exposed to new technology very early. I still remember the first time he showed me the original iPhone. I was in awe of its new technology but most of all the screen. Even now, a screen is what connects us to everything and everyone we love and how we interact with them is immensely important. This is why I'm interested in UX Architecture. Creating experiences that will be useful and impact others positively is my goal.
